ANTHONY REASON

Anthony Reason (b. 1956) was born in Hastings on the South Coast of England. In 1979, Anthony obtained his Degree in Fine Art (Sculpture). In 1980, he started working with bronze casting, utilizing the patination processes. Since the mid 1980’s, Anthony has been producing artworks with metal oxides on canvas. Working with iron, copper, salt and water, he obtains colors that are less controllable while also having greater potential for great vibrance compared to mere pigments. Colors are then bled onto canvas or paper to produce works that resonate with primal energy.
